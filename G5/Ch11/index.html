How To Play

⚖️ Observe Target Pan

The left side of the balance scale holds a mathematical requirement item (e.g., target area or target perimeter).

📦 Load Weight Blocks

Tap the geometric shape block weights in the tray below to load or remove them from the right scale pan. Every block states its size values.

❤️ Balance & Verify

Add up your selected blocks to find the total sum that perfectly matches the value on the left. Click verify to submit your balance configuration.

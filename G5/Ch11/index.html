How To Play

✍️ Draw the Blueprint

Read the task in the header text box carefully. Each square inside the building matrix measures exactly 1 cm by 1 cm (an area of 1 sq cm).

📐 Map Out the Targets

Tap individual blocks on the matrix map to construct the exact area requested in the textbook layout. Tap an active block again if you want to clear it.

❤️ Verify Your Design

Click the build verification button below when you are ready. You have two hearts per grid level before the studio system auto-draws the correct mathematical answer block alignment in green.

How To Play

🪵 Anchor the Elastic

Observe the target area and perimeter rules in the top bar. Tap individual metallic pegs on the wooden board to add elastic anchoring pins.

📐 Stretch Out Layouts

As you tap pegs sequentially, the elastic band automatically stretches to enclose a custom shape. Each step between pegs counts as 1 centimetre.

❤️ Double Constraints

Monitor your active telemetry parameters. Your custom shape must satisfy BOTH the requested area and perimeter values simultaneously before clicking verify!

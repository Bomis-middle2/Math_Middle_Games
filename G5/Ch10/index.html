How To Play

✏️ Paint the Grid

Read the metric scale given in the prompt box (for example, 1 Block = 1 cm). Tap the squares directly on the board to paint your total answer.

💡 Hints and Corrections

If your painted total doesn't match the conversion, a hint will appear. You have two hearts per question. Losing the second heart automatically paints the correct layout in green.

🏆 Confirm Answers

Tap the verify button below the grid when you are satisfied with the number of blocks you have painted to lock in your score.

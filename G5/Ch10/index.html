How To Play

✍️ Draw to Build

Read the textbook task and check the construction scale in the top window (for instance, 1 Grid Block = 1 Litre or 100 Centimetres).

🎨 Paint the Blueprints

Tap individual squares on the empty grid map to paint and construct the structural models. Tap an active block again to erase it.

❤️ Verify & Fix Layouts

Click the build button to test your model. If the dimensions are incorrect, a metric rule pops up. You have two hearts per blueprint before the grid auto-draws the correct asset in green.

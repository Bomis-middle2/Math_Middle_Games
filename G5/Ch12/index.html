How To Play

📦 Read Target Volume

The cargo sheet requests an exact volume space allocation in cubic centimetres ($cm^3$). Pay close attention to whether it's a cube or cuboid structure.

🎛️ Tune Valve Sliders

Drag the Length, Width, and Height sliders to adjust the dimensions of the isometric cargo crate wireframe in real time.

❤️ Safe Packing Bounds

Multiply Length × Width × Height to calculate your active volume. When the active calculation exactly hits the target demand capacity, click verify to launch!
